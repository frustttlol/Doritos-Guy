Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What this covers

This Privacy Policy explains how the Doritos Guy Discord bot and its companion website handle information. Doritos Guy is an independently developed project created to provide moderation and utility features for Discord servers.

What we collect

This website does not collect personal information through its "Dashboard Login" page. The button simply redirects you to Discord and does not authenticate you with this website.

If you invite and use the Doritos Guy Discord bot, the bot may process information supplied by Discord's API in order to function. This may include:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Server (Guild) ID
  • Channel and Role IDs required for bot features
  • Information needed to execute commands and moderation features

The bot never receives your Discord password, payment information, or private direct messages through Discord's API.

How information is stored

This website currently has no backend database or account system. It does not store user accounts or login sessions.

Any data required for the Discord bot to operate is handled separately from this website and only for the purpose of providing the bot's features.

Sharing information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

Information is only processed as required for Discord and the Doritos Guy bot to function.

Your choices

You may stop using this website at any time.

If you have invited the Doritos Guy bot to your server, you may remove it whenever you choose through Discord's server settings.

If future versions of this website introduce real account authentication or dashboard functionality, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may change from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will also be updated.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the official Doritos Guy Support Server .
